About the author

VeraPish is a web app developed by myself (Óscar Iglesias) in the context of an Universitary Information Security Master of Science End of Degree project at UNIR in Spain.

I am an Electrical engineer working for many years in telecommunication operators. Lately, as a Cybersecurity Product Manager in Vodafone Spain. You can check my LinkedIn profile in here.

Perfectly aware of the fact that I am not an outstanding developer, but I thought companies could take advantage of this web application easily. Also perhaps someone else wants to build anything much better on top of that, so I decided to license it as MIT (free to use and modify).

Will be more than happy to hear from you in case you use my contribution or leverage on it somehow, through any of the above commented channels.

Enjoy it and take care!

Óscar Iglesias.
